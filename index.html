The Pig Dice Game

Objectives and Rules

  1. The first player to reach a total score of 100 or more will win!
  2. During each player's turn, they may roll for points as many times as they want to increase their potential turn score which will be added to their total score.
  3. A player can click on the hold button at any point during their turn which ends their turn and adds their turn score to their game score.
  4. If a player rolls a 1 at any point during their turn, their turn is over and they lose any points from that turn that were collected.
